Chapter Mission Statement

The operational directives of Monta Vista Octagon.

Core Objectives

MV Octagon serves as a student-directed community service infrastructure at Monta Vista High School. We organize structured youth leadership tracks through verified civic engagements — ranging from essential resource sorting to public area remediation — ensuring students execute measurable community impacts.

Operational Benefits

  • Accrual of verified service hours authenticated for academic and institution transitions.
  • Direct peer-to-peer accountability and collaborative structural project work.
  • Strategic development of management oversight and clear public service operations.
