About MV Octagon

Learn who we are and why we do what we do.

Our Mission

MV Octagon is a youth-led volunteering organization based in Oakley, CA. We connect young people with meaningful community service opportunities — from food drives to park cleanups — and empower them to create lasting positive change in their neighborhoods.

Why volunteer with us?

  • Build real-world leadership and teamwork skills
  • Earn community service hours for school
  • Meet like-minded peers who care about Oakley
  • Make a direct, visible impact in your community

FAQs

Do I need experience to volunteer?
No experience needed! All of our events are open to anyone who wants to help.
How old do I need to be?
Most events are open to ages 13+. Some may require a parent/guardian.
Will I get service hours?
Yes! We provide signed verification letters for all completed volunteer hours.
How do I cancel a registration?
Email us at support@mvoctagon.com and we will remove you from the event.
Are events free to attend?
Yes, all MV Octagon volunteer events are completely free.
